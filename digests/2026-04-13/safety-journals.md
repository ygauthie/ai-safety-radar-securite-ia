# Journal Articles (2026-04-13)

## Key Articles

**[Interpretability and implicit model semantics in biomedicine and deep learning](https://www.nature.com/articles/s42256-026-01177-0)** - *Nature Machine Intelligence*

This article examines the critical intersection of model interpretability and implicit semantic representations in biomedical AI applications. The research addresses how deep learning models encode domain-specific knowledge that may not align with explicit training objectives, potentially leading to unexpected behaviors in high-stakes medical contexts. The findings have significant implications for AI safety in healthcare, highlighting the need for robust interpretability methods that can detect misaligned implicit semantics before deployment in clinical settings where model failures could have life-threatening consequences.
