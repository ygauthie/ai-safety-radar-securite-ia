3. **Instruction Dilution Demonstrates Safety Fragility**: [Anthropic's evaluation notebook reveals how structured reasoning frameworks achieve near-perfect accuracy in controlled settings but collapse to 0-30% accuracy in complex production environments](https://github.com/anthropics/claude-cookbooks/pull/528), highlighting the critical gap between laboratory safety measures and real-world robustness.
